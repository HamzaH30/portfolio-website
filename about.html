About Hamza

I'm Hamza, a grade 10 student currently attending Pembina Trails Early College (PTEC) & Fort Richmond Collegiate. In my spare time, you can find me playing various sports such as squash, taekwondo, hockey, soccer and many more! I also enjoy biking and volunteering in the community.

I excel and perform very well in academics and maintain a 98% average whilst receiving many academic awards in STEM-related competitions. I am a focused, hard-working and dedicated student with strong work ethics.

In addition to being athletic and focused in academics, I regularly assist in the community - whether it be raising money for charities, babysitting, or teaching programming to aspiring kids.
